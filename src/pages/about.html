Our History

Start of journey 1996

1996

Almost three decades ago, a young former Royal Naval Lieutenant travelled to the Middle East to progress his career in financial services. In the early 90’s, Manama, where he was based was the bustling financial centre in the GCC. However, the UAE was up-and-coming, and lure of Dubai’s bright lights and opportunity was hard to resist. The country had a compelling vision and our founder being a visionary, knew this was where his ideas could become reality. Tim Searle & William Rudge establish Fletcher Young & Associates in Dubai.

Philanthropy

We believe in giving without expectation of reciprocation

We frequently raise funds for great causes and volunteer to help in meaningful projects that change lives, no matter how small. Our company’s purpose is about adding value to people’s lives, which expands beyond our clients to the global community. There are two types of fortune. The first and most important fortune one can have is being healthy, having a home, family and an education. We see inheriting or accumulating a fortune, in the monetary sense, as an opportunity to help those who haven’t experienced the first fortune like we have.
